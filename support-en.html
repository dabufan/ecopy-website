Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How to enable clipboard monitoring?
A: Clipboard monitoring starts automatically when you launch the app. You can control what types of content to monitor (text/images/files) in preferences.
Q: What file types are supported?
A: All common file types are supported, including documents, images, videos, audio, etc. When you copy files, the app will record the file paths.
Q: Where is data stored?
A: All data is stored locally on your device and never uploaded to any server, ensuring your privacy and security.
Q: How to search records?
A: In the record manager, you can use the search box for multi-field search, supporting titles, content, image names, etc.
Q: How to use sidebar mode?
A: Select sidebar mode in preferences, then move your mouse to the screen edge (left or right) to display the sidebar.
Q: How to set global shortcuts?
A: In the "Shortcuts" option in preferences, you can customize global shortcuts to quickly create records or toggle the sidebar.
Q: How much storage does the app use?
A: The app itself is small. Storage usage mainly depends on the number of images and files you copy. You can check storage usage in settings.
Q: What languages are supported?
A: 26 languages are supported, including Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), English, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Russian, Arabic, etc.
